What is Codeflix?

Codeflix is an e-learning platform dedicated to web development. Here, learners will find tutorials on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks, all structured to build real-world projects.

Our Mission

To empower learners with practical coding skills that they can apply to build websites, apps, and solutions that turn ideas into reality.

Target Learners

Our platform is designed for beginners, students, and professionals who want to level up their web development skills in an engaging and interactive way.

How You Will Learn

Through step-by-step lessons, hands-on projects, video tutorials, and interactive exercises, learners will gain confidence in coding while building a portfolio of real-world projects.
